About CCI Akure

A vibrant branch of Celebration Church International, dedicated to spreading the gospel and nurturing spiritual growth in Akure.

Our Mission

Celebration Church International (CCI) is a Pentecostal teaching ministry founded by Apostle Emmanuel Iren, our lead pastor, in 2012. Our vision is to see all people celebrate endless life in Christ Jesus through in-depth teaching, powerful worship, and community outreach. The Akure branch, inaugurated on July 16, 2025, is led by our resident pastor, Pastor Lanre Korede, who is committed to empowering believers to live out their faith boldly.

Located at Celebr8 Center, Quarter 54, Alagbaka GRA, Akure, we are part of a global ministry with over 30 campuses worldwide, guided by the dynamic preaching and teaching of Apostle Iren.
